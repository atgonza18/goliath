DSC Constraints & Production Reports
=====================================
Central repository for constraint tracking and production
reports across all 12 DSC solar projects.

Purpose:
- Track the most critical constraints across the portfolio
- Store reports organized by date for historical reference
- Provide context for Nimrod and all subagents to pull from

Structure:
- /YYYY-MM-DD/ — Daily subfolders containing that day's reports,
  constraint logs, schedule risk snapshots, and meeting prep
- Reports may include PDFs, Excel files, and text summaries

Projects Covered:
- Union Ridge, Duff, Salt Branch, Blackford, Delta Bobcat,
  Tehuacana, Three Rivers, Scioto Ridge, Mayes, Graceland,
  Pecan Prairie, Duffy BESS
